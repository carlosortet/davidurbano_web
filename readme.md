**Carlos Ortet - Executive Resume**

Carlos Ortet is a senior innovation engineer, entrepreneur, and researcher who integrates a deep scientific background in computing with a proven track record in international business, entrepreneurship, and thought leadership. His career spans technical research, global brand development, startup creation, and executive-level consulting, bringing together disciplines as diverse as parallel computing, creative technology, and AI for cultural heritage.

**Education & Academic Background**

- Degree in Computer Engineering, Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB)

Carlos began his formal training in technology at UAB, graduating in Computer Engineering with a strong focus on systems architecture, distributed computing, and early parallel simulation frameworks.

- Master's in Computer Architecture, UAB / CESCA (Supercomputing Center of Catalonia)

He completed postgraduate studies in Computer Architecture, culminating in a thesis (tesina) exploring performance simulation and resource efficiency in multiprocessor systems. His academic involvement at CESCA aligned with its role as a key hub in the European HPC landscape, where Ortet participated in research surrounding load balancing, transputer networks, and parallelism in distributed systems.

- B.Eng. in Electrical Engineering, University College Cork (UCC), Ireland

At UCC, Carlos expanded his engineering foundation, focusing on electronic systems and computational modeling. During this time, he completed a cross-institutional project with Hatfield Polytechnic (now University of Hertfordshire), titled:

- ARGOS: Autonomous Robotic Guidance and Object Sensing System

This project examined object tracking, guidance control, and early forms of sensor integration-critical domains in robotics and industrial automation.

- Master's in Space Science, CESCA (Barcelona) in partnership with the International Space University (Stockholm)

Carlos undertook specialized studies in Space Systems Engineering with a concentration on IT infrastructure, computer architecture, and data systems for aerospace applications. This international program emphasized the computational backbone of space missions, including system coordination and embedded control frameworks.

- Advanced Media Executive Program, IESE Business School (US)

Complementing his technical foundation, he completed the Advanced Media Executive program at IESE, focusing on innovation in digital content, storytelling, and media management skills that he has leveraged extensively in global business ventures.

**Research & Publications**

- Selected White Papers:

Carlos is the author and contributor of several white papers on parallelism, load balancing in distributed environments, transputer-based simulation, and neural networks applied to computational inference. One example is:

- Performance Prediction of Parallel Systems by Simulation - available at academia.edu

This paper explores modeling methods for evaluating the scalability and throughput of high-performance computing systems, which were developed during the author's postgraduate research phase.

- Lecturing & Academic Roles:

Carlos has taught engineering and innovation as a visiting professor and masterclass lecturer at the UAB and other academic institutions in Spain. His lectures have focused on emerging technology trends, AI applications, and digital transformation strategies, constantly integrating his industry perspective into academic contexts. He also directs master's and undergraduate thesis projects in computer mathematics, AI, and data science at UAB and the Artificial Intelligence Research Institute (IIIA-CSIC).

- Recent Thought Leadership:

In July 2025, Carlos published an article in Harvard Deusto Business Review, addressing the intersection of generative AI, strategic brand positioning, and the evolving landscape of human-machine creativity. His publications appear regularly across innovation, tech, and strategic marketing media.

**Executive & Entrepreneurial Leadership**

- Founder & CEO - Zoopa

Carlos is the founder and CEO of Zoopa, a leading innovation and creative technology agency. Over the past decade, he has led the agency's evolution toward AI-enhanced brand experiences, intelligent UX design, and content innovation.

- Founder & Director - 498AS Innovation Lab

He leads 498AS, Zoopa's technology and R&D division. The lab has grown into a global experimentation and prototyping hub, with liaison offices in California (USA) and Australia.

- European Director - Hurley (Nike, Inc.)

Carlos was responsible for the creation and launch of Hurley's European headquarters, where he oversaw market entry, talent acquisition, brand development, and operations.

- EMEA e-Business Director - Nike

At Nike EMEA, Carlos held several leadership roles, including European Automation Director and Equipment Business Unit Director, before being appointed as EMEA e-Business Director. In these roles, he led digital transformation initiatives and built one of Nike's first e-commerce platforms.
